Basic Content

This was the 2nd Theme of the 1st Semester at KEA - Copenhagen School of Design and Technology.

It was a Group Project (5 members). We created a responsive microsite (2 web pages) for "Cycling in Copenhagen" (Topics: Green Cycle Routes & Cycling Guide) and a relative Video to include it on the page .

We designed & created the Prototype, the Moodboard, the Logo, the Style Tile (Typography, color palette, etc.), and then we coded the Microsite using HTML5, CSS3 and JavaScript. Before & after the creation of the Microsite, we did User Tests using the "Thing Aloud Method", and we used the results to make improvements in the UI & UX.

For the Video, after we learned the Language of Film, we created the Treatment and the Storyboard. Then we went for the shooting of the video in Copenhagen (all of us participated). To edit the video we used Adobe Premiere Pro & Adobe After Effects.

Moreover, we did a separate Group Project (3 People) in order to create another Video: "A Story Without Words".
